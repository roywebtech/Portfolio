About Me

I’m Subhajit Roy, an Android Developer passionate about building fast, intuitive, and reliable mobile applications. I enjoy turning complex business requirements into clean, scalable solutions that are easy to maintain and pleasant to use. My primary tools include Java, Kotlin, Flutter, and Node.js, and I’m always exploring better ways to write efficient, high-quality code.

Over the years, I’ve worked on a wide range of products, including payroll systems, POS billing software, pharma field-tracking apps, document management systems, and educational platforms. Each project has helped me strengthen my understanding of real-world workflows, performance optimization, and scalable app architecture.

I care deeply about user experience and believe that great apps should feel simple, responsive, and reliable. I focus on clean architecture, readable code, and long-term maintainability, making sure the products I build can scale as requirements grow. Whether it’s designing smooth user flows, optimizing performance, or integrating backend services, I aim to deliver solutions that add real value.

I enjoy collaborating with teams, learning new technologies, and continuously improving my craft. For me, development isn’t just about writing code — it’s about building products that people actually enjoy using.
